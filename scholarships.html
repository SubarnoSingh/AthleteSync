National Athletic Excellence Scholarship

National Sports Foundation
₹60,000
Eligibility: High school seniors with exceptional athletic achievements and academic record (minimum 6.5 GPA)
Application Period: February 15 - June 30, 2025
Requirements: Athletic resume, academic transcripts, coach recommendation, personal essay

This prestigious scholarship recognizes exceptional student-athletes who demonstrate outstanding athletic ability, academic excellence, and leadership qualities. Recipients may use funds for tuition, books, and other educational expenses at any accredited college or university.

Women in Sports Leadership Scholarship

Women's Sports Foundation
₹30,000
Eligibility: Female student-athletes with demonstrated leadership abilities and community involvement
Application Period: February 1 - May 15, 2025
Requirements: Personal statement, leadership portfolio, two letters of recommendation

This scholarship supports female student-athletes who demonstrate exceptional leadership skills and a commitment to advancing women in sports. Recipients are selected based on their athletic achievements, academic performance, leadership experience, and potential to make an impact in the sports industry.

This scholarship aims to support talented student-athletes from underrepresented communities who face financial barriers to higher education. The program provides financial assistance and mentoring to help recipients achieve their academic and athletic goals.
